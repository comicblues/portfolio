About

I am Eric Schwarz, I work across illustration, animation, branding and storytelling. I've been able to showcase my work at the Lustr festival in Prague, the Pictoplasma conference in Berlin and in New York, where I was selected by the Society of Illustrators for the Best Editorial Illustrations in 2024 and 2026. My clients include The New York Times, Bloomberg, and the Opera of Dresden. My comic books have been published in French, English, Spanish, German, and Ukrainian.
